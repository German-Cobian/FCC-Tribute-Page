ODIN

Image of Odin

  • A God from Norse mithology.
  • The God of wisdom, war and death.
  • Also the God of magic, poetry, prophesy, victory and hunt.
  • Resides in Asgard.
  • Father to many gods, such as: Thor, Balder, Vidar, and Vali.
  • He will lead the forces of gods and men against the forces of chaos.
